    Who We Are

    Sanjay Shah

    President

    Sanjay has over 20 years of experience as CFO and senior level executive in the public and nonprofit sectors, and as a management consultant. He is a published writer and featured speaker on nonprofit finance, business & technology. He was the Co-Chair of Nonprofit Special Interest Group, a sub-group of the Financial Executives Networking Group (www.thefeng.org) for four years from 2004 through 2007, with over 1,400 nonprofit & public sector CFOs and senior financial executives. He holds an MBA degree in Finance from University of Scranton, PA. Sanjay's areas of expertise include: financial management, budgeting, forensic accounting, system/business process improvements, regulatory reports, performance & investigative audits, program development & management, and financial system implementations. A serial entrepreneur, Sanjay loves to take on new, complex challenges and exceed his clients' expectations. He also brings a keen understanding of the pressures and challenges faced by the executives in the public sector.

    Raul Jacoby

    Senior Manager

    Raul has over 30 years of experience as a CFO and senior executive in a Fortune 100 company, audit experience with a big 6 CPA firm, nonprofit organizations and startup ventures. He was the Co-Chair of the Nonprofit Special Interest Group along with Sanjay, a sub-group of the Financial Executives Networking Group (www.thefeng.org) for four years from 2004 through 2007, with over 1,400 nonprofit & public sector CFOs and senior financial executives. He is a New York Certified Public Accountant with a bachelor's degree in Accounting from City College of New York. Raul's areas of expertise include: all aspects of financial management, accounting, budgeting, auditing, and setting up and management of complex projects. A seasoned executive, Raul possesses strong project management, administrative interpersonal and negotiating skills and employs a management style that involves obtaining the respect of peers/subordinates interspersed with a sense of humor to alleviate the pressures of the project deadlines.
